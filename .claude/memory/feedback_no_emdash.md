Never use em-dashes (—) in copy. Rewrite sentences to avoid them entirely. Use colons, periods, or restructure the sentence instead.

**Why:** User has corrected this multiple times. It's a strong stylistic preference across all chapters and content.

**How to apply:** Before writing or editing any body copy, descriptions, or labels, scan for em-dashes and rewrite without them.
