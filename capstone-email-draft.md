Here's what you're looking at:

A multi-chapter interactive framework that models what happens when you build GTM capabilities as a product engineering discipline rather than a content delivery "enablement" function. Capabilities designed like features, shipped through an architecture, measured by behavioral change, and iterated on with data.

The core thesis: **HI^AI = V** -- the quality of human intelligence (base), amplified by the sophistication of AI application (exponent), produces value that neither achieves alone.

There are two primary assets:

1. **Immersive 3D Diagram** -- an interactive Three.js visualization of the full capability architecture stack: contributor layer, product layer, and agent layer. Orbit, zoom, and navigate between views.

This one is built around the CRO value proposition: creating incremental sales-execution capabilities and visualizing impact. A Founder/CHRO variant and CISO/CTO variant will follow, each carrying tailored messaging for their respective buyer.

Everything is interactive. Scroll, orbit, click, explore. It's designed to reward curiosity, the same way the product itself should.
